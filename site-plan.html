Site Name:

  • richmanfedidel.dev

Purpose:

  • To showcase my skills, projects, and experiences as a web developer to potential employers and clients.

Scenarios:

  • A Recruiters/Hire Managers perspective: Does this candidate have the right skills and experience for the role I'm hiring for?
  • A client perspective: Can this developer deliver quality work that solves my business problem?.
  • A fellow developer visits the site to connect and collaborate on future projects.

Color Schema:

  • Primary Color: #4CAF50 (Green) - Represents growth, harmony, and freshness, aligning with the idea of development and innovation.
  • Secondary Color: #FF9800 (Orange) - Conveys enthusiasm, creativity, and success, which are important qualities in the tech industry.
  • Accent Color: #2196F3 (Blue) - Symbolizes trust, reliability, and professionalism, essential traits for a developer's portfolio.
  • Neutral Colors: #FFFFFF (White) and #F5F5F5 (Light Gray) - Provide a clean and modern backdrop that allows the primary and secondary colors to stand out.

Typography:

  • Primary Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - A modern, clean, and highly readable font that works well for both headings and body text.
  • Secondary Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - A versatile font that complements Roboto, suitable for body text and smaller headings.
