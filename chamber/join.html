NP Membership Benefits

Our NP Membership is designed specifically for non-profit organizations to help them thrive in our community.

  • Listing in our online directory
  • Access to member-only events
  • Monthly newsletter subscription
  • Networking opportunities
  • Basic business resources

Bronze Membership Benefits

All NP benefits plus:

  • Enhanced directory listing
  • 10% discount on chamber events
  • Business training workshops
  • Access to business referral network
  • Monthly spotlight opportunity

Silver Membership Benefits

All Bronze benefits plus:

  • Premium directory listing with logo
  • 25% discount on chamber events
  • Priority registration for workshops
  • Featured in chamber communications
  • Access to exclusive networking events
  • Business consultation sessions

Gold Membership Benefits

All Silver benefits plus:

  • Featured placement in directory
  • 50% discount on chamber events
  • VIP access to all chamber functions
  • Quarterly spotlight feature on homepage
  • Dedicated business development support
  • Sponsorship opportunities
  • Board nomination eligibility
